How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complicated setup. Connect your accounts, upload your video, and let CreatorPost handle the rest.

1

Connect your accounts

Authorize CreatorPost with TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram through each platform's official OAuth flow. Connect as many accounts as you need.

2

Upload your video

Drop your video in the dashboard, write a caption, and choose which accounts to publish to. Schedule it or send it now.

3

Set it and forget it

CreatorPost publishes to every selected platform using their official APIs — no browser automation, no manual upload, no phone required.

4

Track your posts

Every published video is logged with a timestamp, platform, and status. Your entire content history in one place.
