How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complicated setup. Connect your account, configure your schedule, and let CreatorPost take it from there.

1

Connect your TikTok account

Authorize CreatorPost with your TikTok credentials through TikTok's official OAuth flow. We only request video posting permissions.

2

Add your videos

Upload directly in the dashboard or point CreatorPost at a folder or API source. Add captions, hashtags, and a scheduled time.

3

Set it and forget it

CreatorPost publishes your video at the scheduled time using TikTok's official Content Posting API — no browser, no app, no manual upload.

4

Track your posts

Every published video is logged with a timestamp and status. Stay on top of your content calendar at a glance.
